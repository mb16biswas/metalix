About METALLIX

METALLIX 2023 IS A MULTI DAY TECHNO-METAL SPECTACLE, INVOLVING STUDENTS OF METALLURGICAL AND MATERIAL ENGINEERING FROM DIFFERENT COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES ACCROSS INDIA WHERE VARIOUS TECHNICAL EVENTS WILL BE HELD. THE EVENTS ARE AIMED AT CREATING A CONGENIAL PLATFORM WHERE STUDENTS CAN INTERACT WITH STALWARTS IN THIS DISCIPLINE, ENGAGED IN RESEARCH AS WELL AS INDUSTRY.

THIS YEAR WE CHOSE TO REFLECT UPON THIS VERY IMPORTANT ISSUE AND CORRESPONDINGLY THE NATIONAL SYMPOSIUM TOPIC IS "CHALLENGES AND OPPORTUNITIES IN NEW AGE MATERIALS DEVEL- OPMENT". WE ARE THRILLED TO HOST THE SYMPOSIUM WHERE EXPERTS FROM VARIOUS SECTORS WILL DELIVER THEIR THOUGHTS ON VARIOUS EXISTING TECHNIQUES, THEIR ADVANCEMENTS AND FUTURE SCOPES TO DEVELOP NEW MATERIALS WITH IMPROVED CHARACTERISTICS. THE STUDENT METALLURGISTS' MEET IS FOCUSED ON HAVING A MEANINGFUL INTERACTION AMONG STUDENTS AND PROFESSIONALS. WE FONDLY ANTICIPATE THE PRESENCE OF EVERYONE TO DELIVER THE SUCCESS OF METALLIX 2023.

FAQ

Will the events held in online or offline mode?

The Seminar and events- Gnosis, Scribe, Talaash and Scroll will be taking place in offline mode only on 24th March, 2023. The Coding competition- Codemet will take place on 26th March, 2023 in online mode.

How to reach the auditorium?

Kindly visit the ‘Venue’ section of our website, which has the location of the auditorium.

What is the registration fees of various events?

Participants can participate in one of Scroll and Scribe for Rs. 50 only. They can also participate in a second event between Gnosis and Talaash for an additional Rs. 50 only. For Codemet, the participation fee is Rs. 70 only.

What is last date of registration?

The last date of Registration in 19th March 2023.

I am from another department of JU/ other colleges. Will I be able to participate in CODEMET?

Yes, you are more than welcome to participate in Codemet and/or any event of your choice!

Will there be cash prizes?

Yes, there will be cash prizes for the winners of the respective events.

Whom to reach out in case any problem arises?

You can contact us through the ‘Contact us’ section in the website or shoot a mail to official.metallixju@gmail.com.

Can a NON-JU student participate in this?

Yes. Any student from any institute or domain can participate in the events.

Will the students of other colleges provided with accommodation?

We, unfortunately are not being able to provide accommodation to students from other colleges, as of now. We regret any inconveniences caused.
